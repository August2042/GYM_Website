About Me

Professional Gym Trainer & Fitness Coach

Hailing from the vibrant land of Rajasthan, I am passionate about transforming lives through fitness and wellness. With over 8 years of experience in the fitness industry, I specialize in creating personalized training programs that help individuals achieve their health goals while building strength, endurance, and confidence.

My journey began in the royal city of Bikaner, where I discovered the power of discipline and dedication through traditional Rajasthani sports and modern fitness training. Today, I combine time-tested techniques with contemporary exercise science to deliver results that last a lifetime.

Whether you're looking to:
• Build muscle and strength
• Lose weight and tone up
• Improve cardiovascular fitness
• Learn proper exercise form
• Develop sustainable healthy habits

I'm here to guide you every step of the way with customized workout plans, nutritional guidance, and the motivation you need to succeed in your fitness journey.
